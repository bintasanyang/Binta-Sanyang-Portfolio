FILE NO. 01 — CAMPUS TRANSPORTATION Delivered

Safe Travels

A project I led end-to-end to fix why campus transportation fails students — scoped, researched, and delivered.

Project Leadership Stakeholder Coordination User Research Prototyping

The Problem

Transportation at the University of Maryland was a daily failure point — unreliable bus routes, unsafe pathways, jaywalking pedestrians, and systems that didn't talk to each other, all compounding into real delays for anyone relying on transit.

My Role

Project Lead — scoped the engagement, directed the research plan, and coordinated the team from problem definition through to a delivered recommendation. — owned the process, not just the findings

Approach

Managed the project through interviews, persona-building, and a campus-wide survey, then coordinated with the team to translate response data into ranked, actionable priorities.

What I Delivered

Directed the build of interactive prototypes testing crosswalk interactions between drivers, bikers, and walkers, then packaged the full research and recommendations into a public-facing website. — usability-tested the prototype with 3 pedestrians and 2 drivers before launch

Survey results on preferred transportation methods around campus — used to identify who would be most affected by any infrastructure changes.

Outcome

A research-backed website presenting prioritized recommendations for improving the campus transportation experience — grounded in real interviews, not assumptions.

FILE NO. 02 — COMMUNITY POLICY RESEARCH Delivered

Equity & Inclusion Meta-Analysis

A consulting engagement I managed for Prince George's County, from scoping through delivered policy recommendations.

Project Management Stakeholder Coordination Survey Design Policy Research

The Problem

Prince George's County needed grounded, community-sourced insight to shape initiatives for its minority youth — who make up 96% of the county's youth population — rather than policy built on assumption.

My Role

Project Manager & Consultant — managed the engagement end-to-end: scoped the research, tracked delivery in Jira, and coordinated the analysis and reporting. — ran the project, research was one piece of it

Approach

Directed the design and fielding of surveys and questionnaires to gather community feedback, then managed the analysis of the resulting datasets in SQL.

What I Delivered

Coordinated the build of visual reports in Tableau translating raw survey data into patterns leaders could act on, with the full engagement tracked and documented in Jira from intake to delivery.

Survey and complaint data analyzed as part of the community research — complaints per 100,000 people by county, and the areas of daily life respondents expect to be most affected.

Outcome

Policy recommendations and visual reports delivered to support minority youth initiatives across the county — grounded directly in what the community said, not secondhand assumptions.
